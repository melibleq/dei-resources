Why It Matters: Surveys and Data

    Wikipedia: Diversity in open-source software
      See the obstacles for inclusion and statistics.
    Digital Ocean Feb 2021 Survey Report
      Contribution to open source decreased in 2020 and could be more ethical.
    Stack Overflow Developer Survey 2021
      Demographics: “91% of all respondents and 92% of professional developers identify as a man”, only 1% as transgender, 58% as white, and other alarming statistics.
    Open Source Survey 2017
      Numbers were already showing the imbalanced demographics and several concerns that the open source community experiences.
    Nature: What does it take to make an institution more diverse?
      Ideas from researchers with different backgrounds and experiences.

      Talk in the 2021 Galaxy Community Conference.
    The Turing Way - FAIR Principles
      Details about the principles and why they matter for community involvement.
    Removing Exclusionary Language in Tech
      Because language is dynamic and can reflect diversity issues.
    Codes of Conduct Matter!
      A discussion on how they contribute for healthier exchanges and relationships in open source communities.
    Asana - Turn impostor syndrome into confidence
      This is an issue especially in underrepresented groups in tech. The post has tips for those who are experiencing it and for managers to help prevent it.
    All Contributors Spec
      A specification to recognize contributions to open source projects beyond coding. The Emoji Key cheatsheet is nice!
